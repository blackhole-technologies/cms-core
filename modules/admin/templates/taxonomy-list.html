About Taxonomies

What are Vocabularies?

Vocabularies are collections of related taxonomy terms. For example, "Categories" or "Tags".

Hierarchical vs Flat

Hierarchical vocabularies allow nested terms (like Categories). Flat vocabularies are simple lists (like Tags).

Using Taxonomy

Assign taxonomy terms to content to organize and categorize your site's content for better navigation and filtering.
