SEO Best Practices

Title Tags

Keep titles under 60 characters for optimal display in search results.

Meta Descriptions

Write compelling descriptions under 160 characters to improve click-through rates.

URL Slugs

Use descriptive, keyword-rich slugs separated by hyphens.

Image Alt Text

Add descriptive alt attributes to all images for accessibility and SEO.

Heading Structure

Use one H1 per page and structure headings hierarchically (H1 > H2 > H3).

Content Length

Aim for comprehensive content. Longer, quality content often ranks better.
