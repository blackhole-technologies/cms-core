Region Tips

Drag to Reorder

Grab the handle (⋮⋮) to drag blocks within a region or move them between regions.

Weight Values

Lower weights appear first. Blocks are automatically reweighted when you drag them.

Disabled Blocks

Disabled blocks remain in their region but won't display on the frontend until enabled.

Theme Regions

Regions are defined by your active theme. Different themes may have different regions available.
