Menu Structure Tips

Drag to Reorder

Grab the handle (⋮⋮) to drag items into a different order or change their parent for nested navigation.

Weight vs Order

Items with lower weights appear first. You can also drag items to reorder visually.

Nested Menus

Create dropdown navigation by making items children of other items. Most themes support 2-3 levels of nesting.

URLs

Use internal paths like /about or full URLs like https://example.com. External links will open in new tabs.
