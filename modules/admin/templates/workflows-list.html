About Workflows

Workflows define the lifecycle states and allowed transitions for content items.

  • States: Represent content status (draft, review, published, etc.)
  • Transitions: Define allowed state changes and who can perform them
  • Initial State: Where new content starts
  • Published State: Content is publicly visible in this state
