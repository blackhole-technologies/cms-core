What are Blueprints?

Blueprints are reusable templates for creating content. They:

  • Pre-fill forms with default values
  • Ensure consistency across similar content
  • Speed up content creation workflow
  • Support dynamic placeholders like {{curlyOpen}}date{{curlyClose}}, {{curlyOpen}}user{{curlyClose}}
