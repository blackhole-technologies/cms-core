About Contact Forms

Form Management

Create multiple contact forms for different purposes (general inquiry, support, sales, etc.). Each form can have unique recipients and confirmation messages.

Email Notifications

Submissions are emailed to configured recipients. Optional auto-reply messages can be sent to users who submit forms.

Submission Tracking

All submissions are logged and accessible in the admin interface. Track unread messages and respond directly from the dashboard.
